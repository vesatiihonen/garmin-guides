Today · Strength

A long, full-body session to bank a strength stimulus before the trip (while traveling it's bands + rope + poles only). Order top to bottom; rest as needed.

Today's order: 1) Aerobic — easy 45–60 min run at ≤165 bpm + 8×10 s hill sprints (walk down). Recovery's green today (Body Battery 76), so the sprints are in. 2) Strength — the session below. 3) Normatec tonight.
Strength stays LOW intensity — 2–3 reps in reserve — the hill sprints are your hard bit, and it's your last full-equipment day before traveling. Tap any move for a demo.

Warm-up

~8 min
01

Slide-board skate

warm-up: 3 min easy · then bands

slide board (+12 kg dumbbell later)

  • Long push side-to-side, soft knees, glide.
  • Then banded lateral walks + clamshells + a few pogos.
▶ "slide board skating"

Legs & posterior chain

3 sets · 2–3 in reserve
02

Goblet squat

3 × 12 · controlled

kettlebell 12 kg (or dumbbell)

  • Chest tall, sit between the hips, knees track over toes.
  • Full depth you own; drive through mid-foot.
▶ "goblet squat form"
03

Romanian deadlift

3 × 10 · slow lower

barbell (~light) or dumbbells

  • Hinge at the hips, flat back, soft knees.
  • Feel the hamstrings load; bar stays close to legs.
▶ "romanian deadlift form"
04

Step-up

3 × 10 / leg

step board + dumbbell 12 kg

  • Drive through the top foot; don't push off the floor.
  • Control the way down. Knee tracks over the toe.
▶ "dumbbell step-up form"
05

Slide-board skate

3 × 45–60 s

slide board + 12 kg dumbbell

  • Push wide, glute-med + adductor drive, glide quietly.
  • Ski-specific — stay low and rhythmic.
▶ "slide board skaters"
06

Calf raise on step

3 × 15–20 · slow, full range

step board (add dumbbell if easy)

  • Balls of feet on the edge, drop the heels below, press tall.
  • 2-sec up, pause, 2-sec down — Achilles loves the slow.
▶ "calf raise on step"

Power & ski-specific

crisp, not heavy
07

Ercolina double-poling

4–6 × 30–45 s · powerful but controlled

Ercolina ski-erg

  • Tall, then crunch the core and drive both poles down past the hips.
  • Full, rhythmic strokes — ski-specific power, low fatigue.
▶ "double poling technique"
08

Kettlebell swing

3 × 12 · snap the hips

kettlebell 8–12 kg

  • Hinge, then hips snap the bell to chest height — arms are ropes.
  • Flat back, glutes fire at the top.
▶ "kettlebell swing form"
09

Pull-ups

3 × 5–8 (or slow negatives)

pull-up bar

  • Chest to the bar, shoulders down, no kipping.
  • Can't rep it? Lower slowly from the top, 4–5 s.
▶ "pull-up proper form"
10

Dips

3 × 8–10 · control

dip bars (or bench)

  • Slight forward lean, elbows back, lower to ~90°.
  • Shoulders down — stop if the front of the shoulder pinches.
▶ "parallel bar dips form"

Core & balance

knee stability
11

Back extension

2 × 12 · no over-arch

back-extension bench

  • Rise to a straight line — don't hyperextend past it.
  • Slow and controlled; posterior chain, not momentum.
▶ "back extension form"
12

Med-ball twist

2 × 12 / side

5 kg medicine ball

  • Lean back, feet up or down, rotate from the ribs.
  • Touch the ball each side; keep the low back supported.
▶ "russian twist form"
13

BOSU single-leg

2 × 30 s / leg

BOSU (dome up)

  • Soft knee, quiet foot — let the ankle/knee do micro-corrections.
  • This is knee-stability gold post-meniscus. Add small knee bends if easy.
▶ "BOSU single-leg balance"
14

Balance board

2 × 30 s · steady

wobble/balance board

  • Keep the rim off the floor; small, calm corrections.
  • Ankle + knee proprioception — finish the session here.
▶ "balance board exercise"

Core finisher (from your drills guide): front plank, side plank, dead bug, Copenhagen — 1 round. Figures + demos in the drills+core guide.
